KEY FEATURES

  Name Search
    - Top of left panel, type any allottee name
    - Case-insensitive, matches partial names
    - Click a result to zoom to the parcel
    - Toggle "Show all [tribe] patents" for context

  Basemaps (Map Display section)
    - Topo: Esri World Topographic (default, detailed)
    - Light Gray: minimal background

  Overlays (checkboxes)
    - Federal Indian Reservations: Census TIGERweb boundaries
    - Rankin Map 1907: historic Crow allotment map (UVA Library)
    - Fee patent density heatmap
    - Individual patents (markers or parcels)
    - Forced fee highlighting (red with yellow border)

  Timeline
    - Check "Cumulative timeline mode"
    - Starts 10 years before first patent (blank slate)
    - Play button animates year by year
    - Speed: 1, 2, or 5 yr/sec
    - Shows trust vs fee accumulation over time

  Analysis (right panel)
    - Summary stats, temporal distribution
    - Trust vs Fee timeline, forced fee rates
    - Conversion velocity, top counties
    - Compare Tribes tab for side-by-side breakdown

DATA
  239,845 allotment patents from Esri Feature Service
  Source: land-sales.iath.virginia.edu (UVA IATH)
  Fields: full_name, preferred_name (tribe), signature_date,
          authority, state, county, forced_fee, geometry
